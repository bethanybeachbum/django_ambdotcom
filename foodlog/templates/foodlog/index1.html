Staying Alive

Health

Proper diet and staying off of pharmaceuticals that doctors easily prescribe is just the start of proper health in retirement

Nutrition

Living well in your retirement is much more than "You are what you eat". That is just the beginning

Wellness

The pursuit of your best possible health and well-being. Make conscious choices that are health-promoting behaviors to achieve your full potential in retirement.

Spirituality

Every day give thanks to God for the long life you have lived. Say your prayers daily and set aside time to be contemplative about how you served God in your life and how you can improve this servitude henceforth.

Giving Back - Gratitude - Volunteerism

How can you give back? Is there a local high school where you can volunteer to tutor students who at risk? A shelter where you can volunteer time to help? Not just a monetary donation, but a donation of your time to give back. This is essential part of fulfillment in retirement." Volunteering gives a deeper connection to your community and others.
